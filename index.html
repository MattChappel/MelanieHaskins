Melanie Haskins

Real Estate Broker & Designer

With both personal and professional experience in residential construction, land development, and design since 2003, I bring a well-rounded perspective to real estate.

My lifestyle-focused planning allows me to see beyond the surface, helping clients unlock the full potential of every property. Whether buying, selling, or building, I provide expert guidance backed by deep industry knowledge and a sharp eye for detail. My goal is to deliver an informed experience that balances both investment value and design vision.

Outside of real estate, I enjoy a good cup of coffee or tea—or occasionally a glass of wine while hosting a dinner party. I have a passion for all things food, especially when they're fresh, local, and in season. I love baking sourdough bread and tending to my garden. I also have a passion for vintage and thrift shopping, where I'm always on the hunt for unique, one-of-a-kind treasures.
